Hi! I’m Maya

Interaction designer with a focus on DEI and accessibility, driven by my IT background to create inclusive digital experiences. Experienced in user-centred design and problem-solving.

Strengths

  • Cross-disciplinary thinking

    My technical support background helps me understand user pain points at a practical level, from troubleshooting software issues to navigating complex systems. This insight enables me to create intuitive and effective interfaces.

  • Ethical approach

    I believe in ethical design - balancing innovation with a responsibility to protect user privacy and ensure that technology improves lives without exploiting vulnerabilities.

  • Fast learner

    I'm always eager to pick up new tools and technologies. My ability to quickly adapt helps me stay ahead in a constantly evolving design landscape.

  • Inclusive design expertise

    I design with accessibility in mind, ensuring that my interfaces are intuitive and usable for everyone. My goal is to create experiences that welcome all users, regardless of their abilities.

  • Interdisciplinary collaboration

    Effective design benefits from strong teamwork and diverse perspectives. I work efficiently with cross-cultural and cross-disciplinary teams, ensuring clear communication and integrating various viewpoints to create innovative and inclusive solutions.

  • Patience

    I approach challenges with patience, ensuring I communicate clearly and effectively, even in complex situations. This ability helps me address user needs calmly and thoroughly.
